Michael LeBron

Born in 1990 into a family of 12, Michael was the youngest child.
Growing up it was hard for him to get any attention from his parents.
That was until highschool when he started gathering attention from everyone
around from his imense basketball talents. His freshman year he averaged 50
points a game.
In 2005, instead of returning to highschool for his sopomore season, he
decided to enter the NBA draft where he would be the youngest number 1 pick
of all time. At 15 years old, Michael LeBron would lead the Seattle Supersonics
to their first and only championship as they were sold the following season
to an Oklahoma City business man.
Averaging 30 points a game in the finals and
winning finals MVP, Michael LeBron decided that basketball was too easy
and he would return to school to get an education. From that point on
his life is no longer exciting or worth making up so the biography ends here.
